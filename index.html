Why Skincare is Important?

  • Keeps skin healthy and glowing
  • Prevents acne and dryness
  • Protects from sun damage

Daily Skincare Routine

  • Cleanser: Removes dirt and oil
  • Moisturizer: Keeps skin hydrated
  • Sunscreen: Protects from sun

Morning Routine

Cleanser → Moisturizer → Sunscreen

Night Routine

Cleanser → Moisturizer

Skin Types

  • Dry Skin: Tight and flaky
  • Oily Skin: Shiny and greasy
  • Combination Skin: Oily nose, dry cheeks
  • Sensitive Skin: Red and itchy

Routine Based on Skin Type

  • Dry: Cream cleanser + Rich moisturizer
  • Oily: Gel cleanser + Light moisturizer
  • Sensitive: Gentle products only

Where to Purchase Skincare Products?

  • Medical stores / pharmacies
  • Dermatologist clinics
  • Trusted cosmetic shops
  • Online platforms (verified sellers only)

Important Tips

  • Always check expiry date
  • Do patch test before use
  • Consult dermatologist for skin problems

Food to Eat for Healthy Skin

  • Fruits: Orange, papaya, apple, watermelon
  • Vegetables: Carrot, spinach, tomato, cucumber
  • Nuts & Seeds: Almonds, walnuts, flax seeds
  • Dairy: Curd, buttermilk
  • Proteins: Eggs, pulses, beans

Hydration

Drink at least 8–10 glasses of water daily.

Avoid

  • Junk food
  • Too much sugar
  • Oily & fried food

Home Skincare Remedies

For Glowing Skin

  • Honey: Apply for 10 minutes, then wash
  • Milk: Clean face with cotton

For Oily Skin

  • Multani Mitti: Use once a week
  • Rose Water: Use as toner

For Dry Skin

Important
